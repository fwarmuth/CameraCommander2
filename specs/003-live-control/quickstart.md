# Quickstart — Live Control & Setup Assist

This guide focuses on preparing your camera and tripod for a shoot using the **Live Control** view.

---

## 1. Framing the Shot

1.  Open the **Live Control** tab in the web UI.
2.  Enable motors by clicking the **Motors** button (ensure it turns green).
3.  Use the **Nudge** arrow keys to position the camera.
4.  The current position is displayed in degrees. Note these for your timelapse start and target positions.

---

## 2. Setting Focus

1.  Observe the live MJPEG stream in the **Live view** panel.
2.  Use the **Focus In** and **Focus Out** buttons (if available for your lens).
3.  For fine-tuning, use small step sizes (±1). For coarse adjustments, use large steps (±3).
4.  Verify focus by looking at high-contrast edges in the live stream.

---

## 3. Verifying Exposure

1.  Navigate to the **Planning** tab in the settings panel.
2.  Set your initial **ISO**, **Aperture**, and **Shutter Speed**.
3.  Click **Test capture**.
4.  The system will perform a full-res capture and display an "Open latest capture" link.
5.  Click the link to open the **High-Res Inspector**. Use your mouse wheel or gestures to zoom in on critical details.
6.  Adjust exposure parameters as needed and repeat until satisfied.

---

## 4. Searching Settings

If you need a specific setting (e.g., "White Balance"), use the **Search settings...** bar at the top of the camera panel to filter the 40+ available commands instantly.
